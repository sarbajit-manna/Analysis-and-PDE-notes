# Analysis and PDE Notes

This repository documents my independent study in analysis, Sobolev spaces, elliptic partial differential equations, and regularity theory.

Rather than reproducing textbook material, I focus on mathematical questions that naturally arise while reading. Each note records the motivation behind the question, my own attempt at resolving it, and the mathematical ideas that emerged during the process.

These notes reflect my personal understanding developed through graduate coursework at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) and independent study.
