# Clarification of the Mathematical Framework

This folder contains a clarification note written following questions raised during discussions with **Prof. Serge Nicaise** (Université Polytechnique Hauts-de-France) about the accompanying research proposal.

## Context

After reading the proposal, Prof. Nicaise asked for clarification regarding:

1. the motivation for the initial graph-theoretic viewpoint, and
2. the role of weighted Sobolev spaces, analytic regularity, and transmission problems in the proposed framework.

The resulting note explains:

- the transition from graph-based modelling to a PDE formulation;
- the role of Sobolev and weighted Sobolev spaces;
- the relevance of boundary singularities;
- the motivation for transmission problems in fiber–fiber interfaces.
